</Note>
## Key Operations
Chargebee performs the following critical operations when processing checkout sessions and creating invoices:
<CodeGroup>
  - Validating customer shipping addresses for tax calculation
  - Estimating applicable taxes for invoices and line items
  - Submitting invoice and credit note documents for tax reconciliation
  - Validating shipping addresses for product delivery
</CodeGroup>
External Service Types
<Card
title="Supported Tax Services"
icon="calculator"


Chargebee integrates with two types of external services:

Tax service providers with calculation APIs
Merchant's in-house tax calculation software
</Card>


Integration Architecture
The integration between Chargebee and tax services is facilitated through a tax service adapter that implements the Tax Service Provider Interface (SPI). This adapter acts as a connector between your service and Chargebee's system.
